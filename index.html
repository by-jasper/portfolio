About

I am an HR professional with deep expertise across the full employment life cycle — from talent acquisition and onboarding through to payroll, compliance, and offboarding. My foundation is built on operational rigour and a thorough understanding of Singapore's HR regulatory landscape including CPF, MOM, IRAS, and WICA.

Beyond traditional HR, I apply a systems-thinking approach to operations — identifying manual bottlenecks and building automated solutions using tools like Python, VBA, Power Automate, and Microsoft Copilot Studio. I believe the best HR teams combine human judgement with intelligent automation.

My work spans from designing end-to-end payroll workflows to deploying AI-powered HR agents that serve hundreds of employees — always grounded in compliance, accuracy, and user experience.
