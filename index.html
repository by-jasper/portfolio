AI & HR Innovation

Building the future of HR with AI and automation.

I strongly believe AI will change the way HR operates in the future.

HR will still require human judgement, empathy, confidentiality, and compliance awareness. However, AI can help HR professionals reduce repetitive work, improve response time, support policy clarification, assist with workflow design, and provide better employee service.

My interest is in practical and responsible AI use — especially in HR operations, payroll, employee query handling, policy knowledge management, and automation.

Responsible AI Use

Use AI without exposing confidential HR data.

  • Do not upload employee names, NRIC / FIN / passport numbers, salary records, medical details, disciplinary matters, or internal documents.
  • Use dummy or anonymised sample data for experiments.
  • Keep human review, compliance checks, and judgement in every workflow.
  • Use AI for drafts, workflow ideas, knowledge structures, and sample code — not final unreviewed HR decisions.
